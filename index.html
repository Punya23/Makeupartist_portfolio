About Me

The Artist
Behind the Magic

With over 8 years of experience in bridal and festive makeup, Dhruvi Surana has redefined beauty for hundreds of brides across India. Her signature approach blends traditional Indian aesthetics with contemporary techniques, creating looks that are timeless, photograph-ready, and deeply personal.

Every face tells a story. Dhruvi listens, understands, and translates your vision into an artful expression of beauty that makes you feel like your most radiant self on your most important day.

Trained in advanced airbrush techniques
Specializes in Indian bridal & editorial looks
Uses only premium, skin-safe products
Available for destination weddings
